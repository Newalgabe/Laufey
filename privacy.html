1. The short version

No analytics. No ad tracking. No account sign-ups. No data sold or shared for marketing. The only thing stored is a small local flag remembering whether you've dismissed the cookie notice - and that never leaves your browser.

2. What we collect

This site does not use analytics platforms, tracking pixels, advertising cookies, or fingerprinting scripts. No forms on this site collect personal information such as your name, email address, or payment details.

3. Local storage & cookies

The only piece of information this site keeps is a preference flag recording whether you accepted or declined the on-page cookie notice. It's stored using your browser's local storage, lives only on your device, and is never transmitted to any server.

You can clear this at any time through your browser's site settings - clearing it simply means the notice will appear again on your next visit.

4. Fonts

Typefaces are loaded from Google Fonts at page load. Requesting them may share your IP address and basic browser details with Google, governed by Google's own privacy policy rather than this one.

5. Third-party links

Social and ticketing links on this page point to external platforms (or are placeholders that point nowhere). Once you leave this site, the privacy practices of that destination apply - not this policy.

6. Hosting & server logs

Like virtually any web server, the infrastructure hosting this page may keep basic technical logs (such as IP address, browser type, and timestamp) for security and reliability purposes. These logs are not used for tracking, profiling, or marketing, and are not shared with third parties.

7. Children's privacy

This site does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children. Since no forms or accounts exist here, no personal data is gathered from any visitor regardless of age.

8. Your choices

  • Clear your local storage at any time to reset the cookie-notice preference;
  • Use browser extensions or settings to block font requests, if you'd rather not load them from Google;
  • Simply close the tab - nothing here requires an account or ongoing relationship with the site.

9. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated as the site evolves. The "last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.
